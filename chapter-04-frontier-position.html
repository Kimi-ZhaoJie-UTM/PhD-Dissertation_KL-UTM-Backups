吉隆坡的"前沿"定位
Kuala Lumpur as the "Frontier" of Islamic Finance

在全球伊斯兰金融网络中，吉隆坡占据着独特的中介枢纽位置，在西方金融惯例与伊斯兰原则、不同教法传统、以及中东资本与亚洲机遇之间发挥转化与协调功能。 KL occupies a unique intermediary position in the global Islamic financial network, mediating between Western conventions and Islamic principles, different Shariah traditions, and Middle Eastern capital with Asian opportunities.
