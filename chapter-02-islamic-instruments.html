Entry Level · 入门级

Murabaha

成本加价

Cost-Plus Sale

  • Fixed predetermined markup 固定利润率 (markup)
  • Debt-based financing structure 债务型融资结构
  • Minimal risk-sharing mechanism 最低程度风险共担机制
  • Resembles conventional loan 类似传统银行贷款
🏢
Intermediate · 进阶级

Ijarah

租赁融资

Leasing Contract

  • Real asset-backed financing 真实资产支持融资
  • Fixed rental payments 固定租金支付
  • Transfer of usufruct only 仅使用权转移
  • Ownership retained by lessor 所有权由出租方保留
📊
Hybrid · 融合级

Sukuk

伊斯兰债券

Islamic Bonds

  • Asset pool backing required 资产池连结支持
  • Variable/uncertain returns 收益浮动不确定
  • Securitized hybrid structure 证券化混合结构
  • Tradable investment certificates 可交易投资凭证
🤝
Ideal Type · 本质级

Mudaraba / Musharaka

利润分享 / 股权合伙

Profit-Loss Sharing / Equity Partnership

  • Authentic profit-loss sharing 真正的盈亏共享
  • Full risk participation by all parties 完全风险参与承担
  • Equity-based partnership structure 权益型合伙关系
  • Ideal-typical Islamic contract 理想型合同范式
