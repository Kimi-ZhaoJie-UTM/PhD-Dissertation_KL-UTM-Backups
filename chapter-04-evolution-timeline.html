MIFC 启动
Malaysia International Islamic Financial Centre
国际金融中心倡议启动，国际定位战略确立，整合监管与人才体系。 Strategic initiative launched to establish KL as global hub, integrating INCEIF and regulatory infrastructure.

绿色 Sukuk 首发
World's First Green Sukuk
全球首支绿色伊斯兰债券发行，可持续融合创新起点，教法合规与ESG目标结合。 Pioneering issuance integrating Shariah compliance with ESG goals, establishing template for sustainable Islamic finance.

1997–1998
亚洲金融危机→强化伊斯兰金融发展承诺，推动金融多元化，暴露传统金融脆弱性。 Asian Financial Crisis reinforced commitment to Islamic finance as diversification strategy.
2013
一带一路倡议启动→为吉隆坡伊斯兰金融带来新机遇与跨境合作空间，创造基础设施融资需求。 BRI launch created new opportunities for KL's Islamic finance ecosystem and cross-border cooperation.
2018–2022
政权更迭→项目调整与制度韧性考验，伊斯兰金融保持相对稳定，NEP嵌入性确保连续性。 Political transitions tested institutional resilience; Islamic finance remained stable due to NEP embeddedness.
