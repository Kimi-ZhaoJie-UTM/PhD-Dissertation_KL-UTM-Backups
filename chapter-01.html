Background & Problem | 背景与问题

Global Trends | 全球金融格局变革

Islamic Finance: Global assets exceeding $3 trillion, prohibition of Riba (interest), emphasizing risk-sharing mechanisms and ethical investment. 伊斯兰金融资产逾3万亿美元，禁止利息（Riba），强调风险分担机制与道德投资。
Belt & Road Initiative: Covering 140+ countries since 2013, infrastructure needs exceeding USD 26 trillion, creating massive financing demands. 一带一路倡议覆盖140多国，基建融资需求超过26万亿美元，创造巨大融资需求。

Critical Case | 关键案例定位

Dual identity: Global Islamic Finance Hub + Key BRI Participant. Interaction mechanisms should be most observable in KL. 双重身份：全球伊斯兰金融中心 + 一带一路关键参与者。互动机制在吉隆坡最为可观察。

Four Research Gaps | 四大研究空白

01 Discursive Fragmentation | 话语割裂
Islamic finance and BRI studies operate in parallel tracks
02 Single-Level Analysis | 单一层次分析
Lack of meso-level framework connecting macro-strategy to micro-market
03 Lack of Case Focus | 案例聚焦不足
Limited systematic case study on KL's internationalization pathway
04 Limited Mechanisms | 机制识别有限
Describing existence of interaction rather than explaining causal mechanisms

Core Research Question | 核心研究问题

How does the interaction between Islamic finance and the Belt and Road Initiative shape and drive the internationalization pathway of Kuala Lumpur as an international financial center? 伊斯兰金融与"一带一路"倡议之间的互动，通过何种具体机制塑造并推动了吉隆坡作为国际金融中心的国际化发展路径？

Core Concepts | 核心概念界定

Islamic Finance | 伊斯兰金融

Financial system adhering to Shariah principles: prohibition of Riba (interest), Gharar (excessive uncertainty), emphasis on risk-sharing, asset-backing, and ethical investment. 遵循伊斯兰教法原则的金融体系：禁止利息与过度不确定性，强调风险分担、资产支持与道德投资。

Belt and Road Initiative | 一带一路倡议

Transnational infrastructure cooperation framework proposed by China in 2013, covering policy coordination, facilities connectivity, unimpeded trade, financial integration, and people-to-people bonds. 2013年中国提出的跨国基础设施与发展合作框架，涵盖政策沟通、设施联通、贸易畅通、资金融通、民心相通五大领域。

International Financial Center | 国际金融中心

Global nodes concentrating financial institutions, transactions, and talents. KL represents the "specialized" type with Islamic finance as core competency. 聚集金融机构、交易与人才的全球节点。吉隆坡代表"专业型"金融中心，以伊斯兰金融为核心竞争力。

Interaction Mechanism | 互动机制

Processes of mutual influence at strategic, institutional, and market levels, with dynamic bidirectional interactions (top-down empowerment, bottom-up feedback, horizontal spillover, recursive reinforcement). 战略、制度、市场三个层面上的相互影响过程，具有动态双向互动特征（自上而下赋能、自下而上反馈、横向溢出、递归强化）。
