Theoretical Contributions | 理论贡献

Original "Strategic-Institutional-Market" framework providing meso-level analytical tool for finance-development initiative interactions 原创"战略-制度-市场"三维框架，为分析金融与发展倡议互动提供中观分析工具

Extending theory to explain how specialized centers interact with external forces (transnational initiatives) 拓展理论以解释专业中心如何与外部力量（跨国倡议）互动
Developmental State | 发展型国家理论

Interaction Mechanisms | 互动机制理论
Identifying recursive institutionalization, legitimation transfer, and network centrality as key mechanisms 识别递归制度化、合法性转移和网络中心性作为关键机制

Key Findings Summary | 核心发现总结

Mechanism 1: Recursive Institutionalization - feedback loops between strategy, institutions, and market 机制1：递归制度化 - 战略、制度与市场间的反馈循环
Mechanism 2: Legitimation Transfer - BRI legitimacy enhances KL's Islamic finance credibility 机制2：合法性转移 - 一带一路合法性增强吉隆坡伊斯兰金融信誉
Mechanism 3: Network Centrality - KL as bridge between Middle East capital and Asian projects 机制3：网络中心性 - 吉隆坡作为中东资本与亚洲项目间的桥梁

Limitations & Future | 局限与展望

Research Limitations | 研究局限
Single Case Study: Limits generalizability to other contexts; findings specific to KL's unique conditions 单案例研究限制推广性；研究发现特定于吉隆坡独特条件
Data Access: Commercial confidentiality constraints limited access to certain transaction details 数据获取限制：商业机密限制了对某些交易细节的访问
Researcher Positionality: Chinese PhD candidate in Malaysia may influence interviewee responses 研究者定位：作为在马来西亚的中国博士生可能影响受访者回答
Temporal Limitation: Study captures snapshot (2024); long-term impacts require future assessment 时间限制：研究捕捉特定时点（2024）；长期影响需未来评估

Future Research Directions | 未来研究方向

  • Comparative Studies: Indonesia, Kazakhstan, and other emerging Islamic finance hubs 比较研究：印度尼西亚、哈萨克斯坦等新兴伊斯兰金融中心
  • Quantitative Analysis: Sukuk-BRI investment correlations and econometric modeling 定量分析：伊斯兰债券与一带一路投资相关性及计量模型
  • Fintech Dimension: Blockchain Sukuk, digital Islamic banking, and technology disruption 金融科技维度：区块链伊斯兰债券、数字伊斯兰银行及技术颠覆
  • Geopolitical Analysis: Impact of US-China relations on BRI-Islamic finance nexus 地缘政治分析：中美关系对一带一路-伊斯兰金融联系的影响
  • Longitudinal Study: 10-year tracking of KL's internationalization trajectory post-2024 纵向研究：2024年后吉隆坡国际化轨迹的十年追踪
Concluding Remark | 结语
KL's pathway demonstrates how specialized financial centers leverage strategic niche positioning and institutional depth to engage with transnational initiatives, creating recursive dynamics reinforcing global status. 吉隆坡的路径展示了专业金融中心如何通过战略利基定位和制度深度参与跨国发展倡议，创造递归动态以强化其全球地位。
"The interaction between Islamic finance and the Belt and Road Initiative in Kuala Lumpur represents a paradigmatic case of how religiously-anchored financial systems can interface with secular development initiatives—offering lessons for South-South cooperation beyond the specific context of Malaysia-China relations."
