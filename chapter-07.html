Sukuk Structure | 债券结构

Structure Type: Ijarah (lease-based) with hybrid Mudarabah elements 结构类型：租赁型（Ijarah）含混合 Mudarabah 元素
Guarantee: Sovereign guarantee from Government of Malaysia 担保：马来西亚政府主权担保
Historical Significance: First government-guaranteed SDG Sukuk in Malaysia 历史意义：马来西亚首个政府担保SDG伊斯兰债券
Key Innovation:
Multi-tranche structure with ESG-linked pricing mechanism - demonstrating innovative blended finance approach 多批次结构与ESG挂钩定价机制 - 展示创新混合融资方法

Risk Sharing | 风险分担机制

Risk Allocation Framework | 风险分配框架

Political Risk | 政治风险
Borne by: Government of Malaysia (via sovereign guarantee) 由马来西亚政府承担（通过主权担保）
Construction Risk | 完工风险
Borne by: CCCC (China Communications Construction Company) as EPC contractor 由中国交建作为EPC承包商承担
Operational Risk | 运营风险
Borne by: Shared between project company (MRL) and operator (future concessionaire) 由项目公司（MRL）与运营商（未来特许经营者）分担
Market Risk | 市场风险
Borne by: Sukuk holders (based on asset/project performance and ridership revenue) 由债券持有人承担（基于资产/项目表现和客流量收入）

Islamic Finance Principle | 伊斯兰金融原则

Unlike conventional loans where the borrower bears nearly all risks while paying fixed interest, Sukuk embodies the Islamic principle of al-ghunm bi al-ghurm (profit accompanies risk). 与传统贷款不同（借款人承担几乎所有风险同时支付固定利息），伊斯兰债券体现了al-ghunm bi al-ghurm（利润伴随风险）的伊斯兰原则。
Key Distinction: Investors are not creditors but asset co-owners, sharing both upside potential and downside risks 关键区别：投资者不是债权人而是资产共有人，共享上行潜力和下行风险

Innovation Significance | 创新意义

First sovereign SDG Sukuk demonstrates KL's leadership in innovative Islamic finance structuring for infrastructure, creating a template for future BRI project financing globally. 首个主权SDG伊斯兰债券展示吉隆坡在基础设施创新伊斯兰金融结构设计中的领导地位，为全球未来一带一路项目融资创建模板。
Replicability: The ECRL model can be replicated for other BRI projects in Indonesia, Central Asia, and Africa seeking Shariah-compliant, ESG-aligned financing 可复制性：ECRL模型可复制用于印度尼西亚、中亚和非洲其他寻求符合教法、ESG对齐融资的一带一路项目
