Core Theoretical Foundations | 核心理论基础

Islamic finance theory is grounded in Shariah principles prohibiting riba (interest), gharar (excessive uncertainty), and maysir (gambling), emphasizing risk-sharing and asset-backed transactions. 伊斯兰金融理论建立在禁止里巴（利息）、哈拉尔（过度不确定性）和麦希尔（赌博）的教法原则之上，强调风险分担和资产支持交易。
