Islamic Finance Theory | 伊斯兰金融理论

Core Tension | 核心张力

Form-compliance vs Essence-compliance (El-Gamal, 2006; Al-Jarhi, 2017) 形式合规 vs 本质合规：部分产品形式上满足教法要求，但实质上接近传统债务工具
Commercial viability vs Religious authenticity tension throughout historical evolution 商业可行性与宗教真实性之间的贯穿性张力

Key Instruments | 核心金融工具谱系

Murabaha (成本加价): Cost-plus financing, most common but criticized as "debt-like" 成本加价融资，最常用但被批评类似债务
Ijara (租赁): Leasing-based, closer to Islamic finance essence 租赁融资，更接近伊斯兰金融本质
Mudaraba/Musharaka (合伙): Profit-sharing partnerships, essence-compliant but complex 利润分享合伙，符合本质但结构复杂
Sukuk (伊斯兰债券): Asset-backed securities, key innovation for capital markets 资产支持证券，资本市场关键创新

Malaysian Model | 马来西亚模式

Dual Banking System: Conventional and Islamic banks operating in parallel with unified regulatory framework 双银行体系：传统与伊斯兰银行并行运作，统一监管框架
Centralized SAC: Shariah Advisory Council providing binding rulings for entire industry 集中化教法委员会（SAC）：为全行业提供具有约束力的教法裁决
Embedded in NEP: Linked to Bumiputera economic empowerment policy, creating political legitimacy 嵌入新经济政策（NEP）：与土著经济政策关联，创造政治合法性

Interaction Logics | 互动逻辑

Top-down Empowerment: Strategic commitment enables institutional development 自上而下赋能：战略承诺推动制度发展
Bottom-up Feedback: Market success reinforces strategic commitment 自下而上反馈：市场成功强化战略承诺
Recursive Reinforcement: Cross-dimensional self-reinforcing dynamics 递归强化：跨维度自我强化的动态机制

Gaps & Theory | 空白与理论

Discursive Fragmentation | 话语割裂

Islamic finance and BRI studies operate in parallel with limited cross-fertilization 伊斯兰金融与一带一路研究平行运行，缺乏交叉融合

Single-Level Analysis | 单一层次分析

Lack of meso-level framework connecting macro-strategy to micro-market 缺乏连接宏观战略、中观制度与微观市场的中观框架

Case Study Deficit | 案例聚焦不足

Limited systematic case study on KL's pathway as specialized IFC interacting with BRI 缺乏将吉隆坡作为专业金融中心与一带一路互动的系统案例研究

Mechanism Identification | 机制识别有限

Describing existence of interaction rather than explaining causal mechanisms 描述互动的存在而非解释因果机制

IFC Theory | 金融中心理论

Specialization strategy (Poon et al., 2016), Path dependence, Frontier position 专业化战略、路径依赖、前沿定位理论

Developmental State | 发展型国家

State-led industrial policy approach in financial sector (Lai, 2015; Rethel, 2021) 金融领域国家主导的产业政策方法
